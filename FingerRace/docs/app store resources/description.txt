Ever want to punch your friend in the face, but worried that you'll hurt your fist? Challenge him to a game of Finger Race instead! Place your fingers (or similarly shaped appendages) on the screen and duel your opponent in this two player game for iPhone and iPad. Do everything in your power to block your friends from reaching the next target but make sure you don't lift your finger from the screen. This thriller will surely solve any quarrel, no matter how significant. Someone might lose a finger along the way, but it's a small price to pay for superiority.
